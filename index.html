Why Did We Build This Project?

In a world where stress sneaks up on us like an uninvited guest (affecting 1 in 5 adults globally), we wanted to create something simple and empowering. Traditional mental health tools‚Äîlike journals or apps‚Äîoften feel too vague or overwhelming.

Wiring: EMG Sensor SIG ‚Üí Arduino A0, EMG Sensor V+ ‚Üí Arduino 5V, EMG Sensor GND ‚Üí Arduino GND. (Optional: Add electrodes to skin for better signal.)

Upload & Test: Open Arduino IDE > Paste code > Select board (UNO) & port > Upload. Open Serial Monitor (Tools > Serial Monitor, baud 9600)‚Äîyou'll see values like "Raw: 512 | Smoothed: 510" every 500ms. Flex your muscle‚Äîvalues spike (e.g., 200-800+).
